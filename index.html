The Wealth & Health of Nations

A Re-Re-Recreation of Gapminder's

This chart visualizes a time series of scatterplots.
Each scatterplot present the income per capita and life expectancy of nations at certain year, and now they are combined into this single display.

Interact to view it lively.

  • Move mouse horizontally on the year number to quickly walk through the years.
  • Hover over the circles to view the history data of the nation.
  • Drag the circles to change the year at your pace.
